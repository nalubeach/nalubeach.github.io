Nalu Beach Club

Our outdoor space offers a relaxed beach vibe with a touch of style, perfect for fun and memorable events by the sea. It can host up to 100 guests for seated meals or 200 for standing parties, making it a great spot for group dinners, company events, or special celebrations.

OCTO

Our elegant interior area offers an intimate setting with plush seating and a more relaxed atmosphere. Ideal for networking events, informal meetings or pre-dinner receptions.

  • Capacity: 50 seated, 100 standing
  • Features: Private bar, flexible seating arrangements, ambient lighting
  • Best for: Networking events, casual team gatherings, cocktail receptions
