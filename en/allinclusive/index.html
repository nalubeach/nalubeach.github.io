Accessibility for Everyone

At Nalu Beach Club, we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to enjoy our unique beachfront experience. We continually work to make our space accessible and inclusive for people with different needs, ensuring that every visitor has a memorable experience.

Our Commitment

We are committed to continually improving our accessibility and inclusivity. If you have any suggestions on how we can make your experience even better, or if you need any specific accommodation not mentioned here, please contact us.

Our staff receive regular training in accessibility and inclusion to ensure that all customers are served with respect, dignity and understanding of their individual needs.
